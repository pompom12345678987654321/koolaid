Best sellers

tropical punch

One of the most iconic Kool-Aid flavors, this vibrant red drink is a favorite for many! Its blend of fruits, including cherries, grapes, and citrus fruits like orange, lemon, and lime, offers a delightful complexity. This is the kind of drink you can enjoy over and over without ever getting bored!

cherry

Cherry, one of the original flavors, continues to be a favorite among Kool-Aid enthusiasts. Its timeless taste is ideal for whipping up a large pitcher to share with friends and family in the sunshine. When you're searching for a delicious summertime refreshment, this flavor is an unbeatable choice!

grape

Despite being one of the more common flavors, it has consistently remained a favorite among Kool-Aid enthusiasts. Its nostalgic and familiar taste brings back fond memories of childhood summers.

blueraspberry lemonade

Although raspberries aren't naturally blue, this flavor is a true gem! Often regarded as one of the top favorites, it offers a delightful mix of sweet and sour that makes it irresistibly drinkable.

orange

This classic flavor is bursting with sugar and a vibrant orange hue. Despite being somewhat underrated, its nostalgic taste often brings back memories.

strawberry kiwi

You cant go wrong with this timeless fruit combination! Kool-Aids take on Capri-Suns fan-favorite flavor is refreshingly sweet without being too intense.
