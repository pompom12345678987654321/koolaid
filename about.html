Edwin Perkins, The creator of kool-aid, The famous inventor, here is the history of his famous invention, kool-aid


the history

kool-aid is founded in hasting nebraska by a man named edwin perkins. perkins was a boy working in his familys general store in Hendley where Perkins became interested in a new powdered dessert mix named jell-o. perkins ends up moving from hedley to hastings as it was a better location for the distribution the products of his compagnie called "The Perkins Products Company". as he is selling these products he sees that fruit smack a bottled soft drink concentrate in six flavors, is his most popular product. the only problem with fruit smack is that the glass bottles were too heavy, too easily broken, and they leaked when transported.

the history (continued)

Therefore By 1927, Perkins had developed a method of removing the liquid from Fruit Smack so the remaining powder could be packaged in envelopes. his product now has a new name "kool-aid". at first kool aid was only sold in candy stores and wholesale grocery stores all over the country. In 1934 it was beginning to be made and distributed overseas. During the Great Depression the price of kool-aid was cut from 10 cents to 5 cents. although the times were tough because of the economic state of the world. kool-aid was still something that many were still able to afford. in 1931 perkins' compagny moved to chicago where they focused on the manufacturing of the drink. in 1953 general foods purchased kool-aid. within a year kool-aid was owned by Kraft Holdings, which markets it with flavors ranging far beyond the original six. this beverage is still and forever will be the official soft drink of nebraska
